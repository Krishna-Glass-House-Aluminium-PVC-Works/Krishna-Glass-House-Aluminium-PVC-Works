Who We Are

Precision Meets Passion

Krishna Glass House was founded in December 2021 by Saksham Bhasin with a simple mission: bring premium-quality glass, aluminium, and interior solutions to West Delhi at honest prices.

Located at Shop No. 2, Mohan Nagar, Pankha Road (opposite D-Block, Janakpuri), New Delhi — 110046, we have quickly grown into one of the most trusted interior hubs in the area, serving hundreds of happy customers across Delhi NCR.

"Every project we take on is a reflection of our commitment to quality. We don't just install glass — we transform spaces."

— Saksham Bhasin, Founder

From frameless glass railings and imported wallpapers to custom LED mirrors and uPVC windows — we offer 8 service categories with a single goal: making your space look extraordinary.
