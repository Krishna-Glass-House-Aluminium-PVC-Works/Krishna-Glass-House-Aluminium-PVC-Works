Glass & Aluminium Office Partitions

Create modern, light-filled workspaces with soundproof glass and aluminium partition systems for offices, coworking spaces, and retail environments.

What Are Glass Office Partitions?

Glass office partitions are modular wall systems that divide open-plan offices into private cabins, meeting rooms, and collaborative zones while maintaining visual connectivity and natural light flow. Unlike conventional drywall, glass partitions give your workspace a premium, contemporary feel.

Krishna Glass House designs and installs complete partition solutions — from single glass walls to full office fitouts with integrated glass doors, aluminium framing, and privacy film. We serve corporate offices, startups, banks, clinics, salons, and retail showrooms across Delhi NCR.

Types of Office Partitions

  • Frameless Glass Partitions — Floor-to-ceiling glass walls with minimal hardware for the sleekest modern look.
  • Aluminium-Framed Glass Walls — Glass panels set in powder-coated aluminium frames for structural strength and design flexibility.
  • Frosted / Privacy Glass — Sandblasted, frosted, or vinyl-film glass for meeting rooms, HR cabins, and director offices.
  • Double-Glazed Acoustic Partitions — Two glass panels with an air gap for maximum sound insulation (up to 42 dB).
  • Demountable Systems — Partitions that can be disassembled and relocated when your office layout changes.

Key Benefits

  • ✓ Natural Light — Glass partitions preserve daylight across the entire floor, reducing energy costs
  • ✓ Sound Insulation — Acoustic glass options reduce noise by 35-42 dB
  • ✓ Professional Aesthetic — Creates a premium, modern workspace that impresses clients and employees
  • ✓ Quick Installation — Faster than drywall construction, with no dust or debris
  • ✓ Customisable — Logos, patterns, and brand colours can be etched, printed, or vinyl-applied
  • ✓ Reconfigurable — Demountable systems can be moved as your office grows
