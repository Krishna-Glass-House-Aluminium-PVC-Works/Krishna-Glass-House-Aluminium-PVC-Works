PVC Wall Panels & Designer Wallpapers

Transform any room with 100% waterproof PVC panels and imported designer wallpapers. Quick installation, low maintenance, stunning results.

What Are PVC Wall Panels & Designer Wallpapers?

PVC wall panels are pre-fabricated interlocking sheets made from premium-grade Polyvinyl Chloride. They offer a clean, seamless wall finish that mimics marble, wood, stone, and other luxury materials — without the weight, cost, or maintenance of the real thing. They are 100% waterproof, making them ideal for bathrooms, kitchens, balconies, and commercial spaces.

Designer wallpapers add character and visual drama to any room. At Krishna Glass House, we stock imported European and Korean wallpaper collections featuring floral patterns, geometric prints, textured finishes, damask designs, and contemporary abstract art. Our wallpapers are peel-proof, UV-stable, and easy to clean.

Whether you are refreshing a single accent wall or cladding an entire office, we offer a vast catalogue of designs and finishes with same-day installation available for standard rooms.

Benefits of PVC Panels & Wallpapers

  • 100% Waterproof — Perfect for wet areas: bathrooms, kitchens, laundry rooms
  • No Painting Required — Installs directly over existing walls without plastering or priming
  • Termite & Pest Resistant — Unlike wood, PVC is immune to termites and insects
  • Low Maintenance — Wipe clean with a damp cloth. No re-painting needed
  • Quick Installation — A standard room can be done in 4-6 hours
  • 15-20 Year Lifespan — UV-resistant, scratch-resistant, and colour-fast
  • Eco-Friendly Options — Recyclable PVC and non-toxic adhesives available

Our Installation Process

1

Design Consultation

Browse our catalogue of 500+ designs. We help you choose the right colours, textures, and patterns for your space.

2

Measurement & Estimation

We measure your walls and calculate the exact material needed — no wastage, no surprises.

3

Surface Preparation

For PVC: install a framework if needed. For wallpaper: clean and prime the wall surface.

4

Installation

Panels are cut to size and interlocked, or wallpaper is applied with professional-grade adhesive for a bubble-free finish.

5

Final Touch-Up

Edge trims, corner moulds, and finishing strips are applied for a seamless, polished look.
