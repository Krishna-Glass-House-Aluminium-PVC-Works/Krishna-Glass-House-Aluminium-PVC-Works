Designer LED Mirrors

Premium touch-sensor backlit LED mirrors for bathrooms, salons, dressing rooms, and modern interiors. Energy-efficient, elegant, and available in custom sizes.

What Are LED Mirrors?

LED mirrors are stylish wall-mounted mirrors with integrated LED lighting behind or around the glass. Unlike traditional vanity lights that cast harsh shadows, LED mirrors provide even, diffused illumination that is perfect for grooming, makeup application, and bathroom use.

At Krishna Glass House, we supply and install a premium range of LED mirrors featuring touch-sensor controls, anti-fog technology, and dual colour temperature (warm 3000K / cool 6500K). Our mirrors are available in rectangular, round, oval, arch, and custom shapes — from compact vanity mirrors to full-length dressing mirrors.

Whether you are designing a luxury bathroom, fitting out a salon, or upgrading a hotel washroom, our LED mirrors add a sophisticated, high-end touch to any interior.
