Glass Railings & Balustrades

Frameless and semi-frameless toughened glass railings that combine maximum safety with unobstructed views.

What Are Glass Railings?

Glass railings (also called glass balustrades) use large panels of toughened safety glass as a barrier for balconies, staircases, terraces, pool areas, and mezzanines. Unlike traditional metal or wood railings, glass provides an unobstructed view, makes spaces feel larger, and adds a contemporary, luxurious aesthetic.

At Krishna Glass House, we install frameless and semi-frameless glass railing systems using 10mm and 12mm Saint-Gobain and Modiguard toughened glass. All hardware is marine-grade stainless steel (SS 304/316) for corrosion resistance and longevity.

Types of Glass Railings

  • Frameless Glass Railings — Glass panels fixed to base channels or spigots with no visible framing. The purest, most minimalist look.
  • Semi-Frameless Railings — Glass panels with a stainless-steel top-rail handrail for added grip and design accent.
  • Post-Mounted Glass Railings — Glass panels between stainless-steel posts. Ideal for decks and poolside areas.
  • Glass Staircase Railings — Custom-cut panels that follow the slope of your staircase with precision-machined fittings.

Our Installation Process

1

Site Measurement

We measure every running foot of your balcony, staircase, or terrace with millimetre precision.

2

Design Selection

Choose frameless, semi-frameless, or post-mounted style. Select glass type (clear/frosted/tinted) and hardware finish.

3

Glass Fabrication

Panels are custom-cut, toughened, edge-polished, and drilled (if needed) at our facility.

4

Installation

Base channels/spigots are anchored, glass panels are fitted with precision, and all connections are sealed.

5

Safety Inspection

Load testing, stability check, and final cleaning before handover. Every installation meets safety codes.
