Custom Glass Facades

Precision-engineered structural glass exteriors for modern residential and commercial buildings across Delhi NCR.

What Are Custom Glass Facades?

A glass facade is a non-structural outer covering of a building that uses large panels of toughened or laminated glass mounted on aluminium or steel frameworks. Glass facades transform conventional buildings into modern, light-filled spaces while offering superior thermal insulation, UV protection, and acoustic performance.

At Krishna Glass House, we specialise in designing, fabricating, and installing custom glass facade systems for offices, showrooms, retail fronts, residential villas, and high-rise buildings. We exclusively use Saint-Gobain and Modiguard glass — both industry-leading brands trusted for exceptional clarity, safety, and durability.

Whether you need a full curtain wall for a commercial tower or a spider-glazed storefront for your retail space, our team in Janakpuri delivers end-to-end solutions — from structural engineering to final installation.

Types of Glass Facades We Install

  • Curtain Wall Systems — Aluminium-framed glass walls that hang from the building structure like a curtain, ideal for high-rise buildings and commercial towers.
  • Spider Glazing — Minimalist point-fixed glass systems using stainless-steel spider fittings for a frameless, seamless appearance.
  • Structural Glazing — Silicone-bonded glass panels with no visible framing, creating a sleek monolithic glass surface.
  • Point-Fixed Glazing — Glass panels secured with bolted fittings through drilled holes, offering maximum transparency.
  • Frameless Glass Storefronts — Full-glass shop fronts for retail showrooms and boutiques with maximum product visibility.

Our Installation Process

1

Consultation & Site Visit

We visit your location, understand your requirements, take precise measurements, and discuss design preferences.

2

Design & Engineering

Our team creates detailed drawings, selects the right glass type and thickness, and engineers the support structure.

3

Fabrication

Glass panels are cut, toughened, and edge-polished to exact specifications. Aluminium frameworks are powder-coated.

Installation
