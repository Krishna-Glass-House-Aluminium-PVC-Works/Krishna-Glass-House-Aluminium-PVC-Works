Aluminium & uPVC Windows & Doors

Premium weather-resistant, sound-insulating window and door systems for homes, offices, and commercial spaces across Delhi NCR.

What Are Aluminium & uPVC Windows?

Aluminium and uPVC (unplasticised Polyvinyl Chloride) windows and doors are the modern standard for residential and commercial buildings. Unlike traditional timber frames, these profiles offer superior weather resistance, virtually zero maintenance, and decades of reliable performance.

Krishna Glass House supplies and installs a complete range of aluminium and uPVC window and door systems — from compact bathroom windows to expansive floor-to-ceiling sliding doors for penthouses and showrooms. Our systems are precision-fabricated, factory-sealed, and installed by trained technicians for a perfect fit every time.

Whether you are renovating a flat in Janakpuri, fitting out a new office in Dwarka, or building a villa in Gurgaon, we have the right window system for your project and budget.

Window & Door Types We Offer

  • Sliding Windows — Smooth-glide 2-track and 3-track systems for balconies, bedrooms, and shops. Space-saving and easy to operate.
  • Casement Windows — Side-hinged windows that swing outward for maximum ventilation and unobstructed views.
  • Tilt & Turn Windows — Dual-action windows that tilt inward for ventilation or open fully for cleaning. Popular in modern apartments.
  • Folding / Bi-Fold Doors — Multi-panel doors that fold like an accordion, connecting indoor and outdoor spaces seamlessly.
  • Sliding Doors — Heavy-duty lift-and-slide systems for living rooms, balconies, and commercial entrances.
  • French Doors — Classic double-swing doors for elegant room dividers and balcony access.
  • Fixed Glass Panels — Non-operable glass panels for feature walls and display windows.

Aluminium vs uPVC — Which Should You Choose?

AluminiumSlimmer profiles, lightweight, corrosion-resistant, ideal for large openings
uPVCSuperior heat insulation, maintenance-free, excellent noise reduction
BudgetAluminium is cost-effective; uPVC is a premium investment

Our Installation Process

1

Free Site Visit & Measurement

We visit your location, measure every opening, and understand your ventilation, security, and aesthetic needs.

2

Profile & Glass Selection

Choose from aluminium or uPVC profiles. Select glass type (clear, tinted, frosted, or double-glazed) and hardware finish.

3

Fabrication

Windows and doors are precision-cut, welded (uPVC) or crimped (aluminium), glazed, and sealed in our facility.

Installation & Sealing

Our technicians install each unit with plumb-level accuracy, apply weather sealant, and test every mechanism.

Final Inspection

Quality check on operation, locking, drainage, and finish. We clean up and hand over a finished product.
