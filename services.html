Exterior Solutions

Custom Glass Facades

Our custom glass facades are engineered to create breathtaking building exteriors that maximize natural light while providing superior thermal and acoustic insulation. We work with industry-leading glass suppliers to deliver curtain walls, spider glazing, and structural glass systems tailored to your architectural vision.

Curtain Walls Spider Glazing Structural Glass Thermal Insulation UV Protection
Windows & Doors

Aluminium Windows & Doors

Experience the perfect balance of aesthetics and performance. Our aluminium window and door systems feature premium thermal-break profiles that ensure superior energy efficiency, weatherproofing, and durability — all while maintaining slim sightlines for maximum glass area.

Sliding Systems Casement Windows Folding Doors Thermal Break Sound Insulation
Interior Finishes

PVC Panel Wall Cladding

Transform your interiors with our premium PVC wall cladding solutions. Available in a wide range of finishes — from marble effects to wood grains — our panels are 100% waterproof, easy to maintain, and install quickly with minimal disruption to your daily routine.

Waterproof Marble Finishes Wood Grain Easy Install Low Maintenance

Glass Railings & Balustrades

Our frameless and semi-frameless glass railing systems combine unobstructed views with maximum safety. Using toughened and laminated safety glass with premium stainless steel or aluminium hardware, we create elegant boundary solutions for balconies, staircases, and terraces.

Frameless Design Tempered Glass SS Hardware Balcony Systems Staircase Rails
